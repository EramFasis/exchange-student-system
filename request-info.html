Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for the exchange program?

Undergraduate students with good academic standing are eligible.

How long does an exchange last?

Most exchanges last 1 semester, but some programs allow 1 full year.

Is housing included?

It depends on the host university. Some offer campus housing, others require outside booking.

Do I need a specific GPA?

Most programs require a minimum 2.5–3.0 GPA.
