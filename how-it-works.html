Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the exchange program last?

Most programs last one semester or one full academic year.

Can I apply to more than one university?

Yes, you can select multiple preferred universities in the application form.

Will I receive financial support?

Scholarships and financial assistance depend on your home institution.
